# Product Definition - Mystical Tao

## Initial Concept
A beautiful and meditative Yin Yang (Tao) animation for the KDE Plasma 6 desktop, combining ancient symbolism with modern aesthetics through a dynamic, C++-powered particle system and an optional mystical clock overlay.

## Target Audience
- **KDE Plasma 6 Enthusiasts:** Users seeking high-quality, aesthetic, and meditative enhancements for their desktop environment.
- **Clock Seekers:** Users who want a unique, functional, and visually striking (neon-lit) timekeeping widget.
- **Plasma Developers:** A reference implementation for creating performant Plasma 6 widgets that leverage C++ plugins for complex animations.

## Core Features
- **High-Performance Particle System:** A custom C++ engine (`taoplugin`) that handles dense particle simulations with dynamic movement and collision, ensuring smooth animation even at high particle counts.
- **Mystical Neon Clock:** An optional, highly customizable clock overlay featuring neon-lit hands and a design that complements the central Tao symbol.
- **Zen Aesthetics:** A slowly rotating, visually balanced Yin Yang symbol designed to bring a sense of peace and focus to the workspace.
- **Extensive Customization:** User-configurable settings for particle density, speed, colors, and clock appearance via a native Plasma configuration interface.
